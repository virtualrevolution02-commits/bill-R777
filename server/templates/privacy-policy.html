Privacy Policy

How we handle and protect your information

Effective: March 15, 2026
1

Introduction

Welcome to Ragu Auto Works ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Ragu Auto Works – Sales & Service (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this policy, please do not use the App.

2

Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of information:

  • Customer Details: Name, phone number, address, Instagram ID, and vehicle information that you enter while creating invoices.
  • Service & Billing Data: Bill numbers, item lists, prices, labour charges, advance payments, odometer readings, and invoice PDF documents.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for app functionality and diagnostics.
  • Usage Data: App interaction patterns (e.g., features accessed, screens visited) to improve user experience.
3

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • Core App Features: Generating invoices, managing parts inventory, tracking service history, and calculating earnings.
  • Bill Sharing: Forwarding invoices to customers via WhatsApp or other sharing methods you initiate.
  • Data Persistence: Storing bills locally on your device and optionally syncing to our server for backup.
  • Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance features, fix bugs, and improve performance.
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting applicable legal obligations and resolving disputes.
4

Data Storage & Security

Your data is primarily stored locally on your device using on-device storage. When server-side persistence is enabled, data is transmitted over secure channels.

We implement commercially reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

🔒 We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

5

Third-Party Services

The App may integrate with or use the following third-party services:

  • WhatsApp: For forwarding invoices to customers. Data shared is governed by WhatsApp's own privacy policy.
  • Expo / React Native: Framework used to build the App. Expo may collect anonymous diagnostic data.
  • Google Play Services: For app distribution and updates on Android devices.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

6

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

7

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data from our systems.
  • Data Portability: Request a machine-readable copy of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.
