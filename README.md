# Ragu Auto Works

A comprehensive billing and management solution for Ragu Auto Works, designed to streamline shop operations, track service history, and generate professional invoices in a standard A4 format.

## 🚀 Key Features

### 📋 Billing & Invoicing

- **Sequential Bill Numbering**: Automated generation of formatted bill numbers (e.g., 001, 002, 003).
- **A4 Formatted Bills**: Every bill is exported in a professional standard A4 aspect ratio, perfect for sharing and printing.
- **WhatsApp Integration**: Seamlessly forward bills directly to customers via WhatsApp.
- **Detailed Tracking**: Record specific service data including Odometer readings (Last Service & Next Service) with automatic "km" formatting.
- **Labour & Advance Tracking**: Easily manage labour costs and track advance payments to calculate accurate final balances.

### 🔍 Inventory & Data

- **Massive Parts Database**: Instant access to a searchable database of over **100,000 items**.
- **Dynamic Item Entry**: Quickly add new spare parts or custom labour items to any bill.
- **Manual Price Control**: Flexibility to adjust prices manually for specific situations.

### 🏢 Business Presence

- **Comprehensive Shop Branding**: Professional inclusion of owner name, Instagram ID (@dr._duker), business address, and contact numbers on every invoice.
- **"Sales & Service" Focus**: Unified branding across the app to reflect both parts sales and garage services.

### 📜 History & Management

- **Bill History**: Complete log of all past bills with a detailed view for each.
- **Edit Capability**: Ability to revisit and update existing bills from the history section.
- **Earnings Tracking**: Monitor business performance through integrated earnings data.

## 📱 Tech Stack

- **Framework**: React Native with Expo
- **Navigation**: Expo Router
- **State Management**: GarageContext Provider
- **Persistence**: Local storage for offline-first bill management
